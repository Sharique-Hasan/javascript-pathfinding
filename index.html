Tile Stats Overview

F = Calculated Total
Sum of the heuristic (H) and current step cost (G)
G = Step Cost
Total number of steps required to walk here from the start tile.
H = Heuristic Estimate
To get the Heuristic an equation is used. It attempts to measure the distance from the current tile to the goal.
